# Episode 062: Port (Ruby / LBV / Tawny) - The Portuguese Fire

## [WSET L2 Practice Question]

**Q. What is the characteristic flavor and color profile of 'Tawny Port' from the 'Douro' valley in Portugal, gained through its oxidative aging process?**

A. Deep ruby color and fresh strawberry flavors (Deep ruby/Fresh strawberry)
B. **Tawny color, walnut, toffee, caramel**, and prune (dried fruit) flavors
C. Pale green color and zesty lime flavors (Pale green/Lime)
D. Changes any wine into a shot of soju instantly.

---

## [Answer & Explanation]

**Answer: B. Tawny color, walnut, toffee, caramel, and prune flavors**

**Explanation**: **Port** is a fortified wine made by adding a 77% ABV grape spirit during fermentation to kill the yeast and preserve natural sweetness. **Ruby Port** is aged in large vessels to maintain fresh black fruit character. **LBV (Late Bottled Vintage)** is made from a single year's harvest and offers deeper concentration. Conversely, **Tawny Port** undergoes deliberate oxidative aging in smaller oak barrels, turning brown and gaining complex notes of **Walnut, Toffee, and Caramel**.

---

## [Expert Concept]

A summary of Port wine styles:

1.  **Ruby Port**: The most accessible style. (Deep ruby, Sweet, Fresh black fruit).
2.  **LBV (Late Bottled Vintage)**: Aged for 4-6 years before bottling. Offers smoother tannins and richer fruit than standard Ruby.
3.  **Tawny Port**: The pinnacle of oxidative aging. (Tawny/Brown color, Nutty, Toffee, Dried fruit).
4.  **Vintage Port**: Produced only in exceptional years and aged in the bottle for decades. (Complexity, Intense fruit, Sediment).
5.  **Fortification**: All Port is fortified **during** fermentation, so **'Sweetness'** is its fundamental trait.

---

## [Exam Tip & Trap]

**[Trap]**: Do not think that Tawny Port is fortified after fermentation is complete. Port is fortified **mid-fermentation** to retain natural sugar—a key difference from Sherry.
**[Tip]**: In the exam, link **'Oxidative aging/Tawny color'** with Tawny Port, and **'Deep color/Black fruit'** with Ruby Port.
